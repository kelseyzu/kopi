Monsoon

low acid blend

brown rice, cereal, peanut

“Monsooning”: The process of transporting green coffee to the Malabar coastal city of Mangalore to be spread out on large covered patios where the coffee can slowly absorb moisture, causing it to swell and turn white. It is then placed in warehouses where it is constantly moved until the moisture is reduced to 11% for exportation.

best for: pour over & espresso

region: kintamani highlands, bali, indonesia

process: sun dried natural
